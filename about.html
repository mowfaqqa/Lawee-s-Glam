About Us

Lawee's Glam offers unique make-up services at affordable rates. We enhance the beauty of womeen of all colors and age. We are a cosmetics organisation involved in make-ups, skin care treatments, sales of beauty products and lots more . Our services apply to weddings, birthdays, parties, photoshoots, day to day casual make-up and any other occassions or events. At lawee's we provide our clients with the most efficient services using very quality cosmetic products available giving our customers a qualitative and satisfactory outcome, in essense, we aim to please to the fullest of our abilities.

Lawee's Glam is located in Abuja, Nigeria. We are also known nation-wide and provide our services around all parts of the country.
